Configure associated domains (iOS, tvOS, watchOS)

Enable associated domains if you want your app to be associated with a domain to access specific servicesâ€”such as Safari saved passwords and activity continuation.

  1. In the project editor, select the target, then click Capabilities.

  2. In the Associated Domains section, click the switch to turn it from OFF to ON.

  3. At the bottom of the Domains table, click the Add button (+).

  4. Double-click the placeholder text in the table, and enter the domain name you want to add.
