Take a screenshot on a device

If you need a launch image for your app, you can use Xcode to launch an app on a device and capture a screenshot. For iOS and tvOS apps, connect a device to your Mac before you begin. For watchOS apps, connect an iPhone paired with an Apple Watch.

Take a screenshot using the Debug menu

  • Choose Debug > View Debugging > Take Screenshot of [Device Name].

    The new screenshot appears on the desktop.

Take a screenshot using the Devices and Simulators window

  1. Open the Devices and Simulators window (choose Window > Devices and Simulators), then click Devices.

  2. In the left column, select your device, and in the detail pane, click Take Screenshot.

    The new screenshot appears on the desktop.
