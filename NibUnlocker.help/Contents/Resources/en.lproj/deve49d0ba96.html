Configure background modes (iOS, tvOS, watchOS)

Enabling background modes allows your app to continue running in the background.

  1. In the project editor, select the target, then click Capabilities.

  2. In the Background Modes section, click the switch to turn it from OFF to ON.

  3. Optionally, select the supported modes from the checkboxes below.

    Xcode adds the background modes you select to the information property list.
