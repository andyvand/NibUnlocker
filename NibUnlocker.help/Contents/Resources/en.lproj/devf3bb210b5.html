Use tabs in the main window

You can reduce clutter on your desktop by opening window layouts in tabs instead of separate window.

  • Show all tabs: Choose View > Show Tab Bar.

  • Add a tab: Click the New Tab button (+) in the tab bar or choose File > New Tab (Command-T).

  • Move between tabs: Click a tab.

  • Reorder tabs: Drag a tab left or right.

  • Close a tab: Move the pointer over the tab, then click the Close button X.

  • Open tab in a window: Drag a tab to another tab bar or to the desktop.
