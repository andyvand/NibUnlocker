View Apple Watches paired with an iPhone

In the Devices and Simulators window, you can view the Apple Watches paired with a connected iPhone.

  1. Connect an iPhone that is paired with an Apple Watch to your Mac.

  2. Open the Devices and Simulators window, then click Devices.

  3. In the left column, select the iPhone device.

    The Apple Watches paired with the iPhone appear in the Paired Watches table.
