Distribution signing options

Choose whether to re-sign your app using Xcode-managed distribution provisioning profiles or ones that you create yourself.

Note: If you manually sign an app in the archive, “Automatically manage signing” is not a distribution signing option when exporting your app.

Option

Description

Automatically manage signing

Xcode creates distribution certificates and provisioning profiles for you.

Manually manage signing

You create your own distribution certificates and provisioning profiles in your developer account, download the provisioning profiles, and assign them to targets in your app.
