Layer views

Change the layer of a view to make it appear in front of or behind sibling views.

  1. On the Interface Builder canvas or in the outline view, select the desired view.

  2. Choose the appropriate command from the Arrange submenu in the Editor menu.

    Send to Front: Move the view to the top layer, placing it in front of all of the sibling views.

    Send to Back: Move the view to the bottom layer, placing it in behind all of the sibling views.

    Send Forward: Move the view up one layer.

    Send Backward: Move the view down one layer.

The depth order of views in a superview impacts both the visual appearance and behavior of an app. Visually, views at the front, or top, are drawn on top of the views below them.
