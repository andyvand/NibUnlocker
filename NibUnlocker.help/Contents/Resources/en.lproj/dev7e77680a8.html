Refactor a storyboard into multiple storyboards

Refactor your storyboard to make implementation and maintenance of the visual layout easier, to encapsulate scenes that correspond to view controllers, and to more easily share storyboards between projects.

  1. On the storyboard, select the scenes to refactor into a new storyboard.

  2. Choose Editor > Refactor to Storyboard.

  3. In the dialog that appears, enter a name for the new storyboard and click Save.

    A new storyboard is created, the selected scenes are moved to that storyboard, and the new storyboard opens in the editor.

    On the original storyboard, the refactored scenes are replaced by a storyboard reference to the new storyboard.
