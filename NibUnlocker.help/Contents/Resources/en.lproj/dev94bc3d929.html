Suspend and resume threads

Use the process view in the Debug navigator to suspend and resume threads. The Debug navigator opens automatically when you pause the execution of your app (click the Pause button in the debug area) or your app reaches a breakpoint in the code. To open the Debug navigator yourself, click the Debug Navigator button () in the navigator area.

  1. In the navigator area, click the Debug navigator button () in the navigator bar.

  2. Command-click a thread from the process view and choose Suspend Thread from the pop-up menu to suspend a running thread.

  3. Command-click a thread from the process view and choose Resume Thread from the pop-up menu to resume a suspended thread.
