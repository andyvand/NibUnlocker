About Devices and Simulators window

Use the Devices and Simulators window to manage devices and simulators that appear as run destinations in the Scheme menu in the toolbar. From the window, you can pair and set up network devices. You can inspect and change the contents of devices. You can install apps, view crash logs, and take screenshots. You can also configure new simulators and pair Apple Watch simulators with iPhone simulators.

To open the Devices and Simulators window, choose Window > Devices and Simulators.

The Devices and Simulators window is divided into two areas. The column on the left contains a list of the devices or simulators. The detail area on the right shows information about the selected device or simulator, including the name, the OS version, and model. For real devices, the detail area shows the device ID (identifier) and memory capacity. To switch between the devices and simulators, click the toggle at the top of the window.

Actions you can perform on the Devices pane include:

Set up a device: Under the left column, click the Add button (+). In the sheet that appears, choose a device, then click Next.

Show or hide a device as a run destination in the Scheme menu: Select a device, then in the detail area, select Show as run destination.

Show the device console: Select a connect device, and in the lower-left corner of the detail pane, expand the disclosure triangle. To clear the device console, click the trash can. To save the output of the console, click the arrow icon adjacent to the trash can. For an iPhone paired with an Apple Watch, click the name of the device to toggle between them.

Filter the list of devices: Enter text in the filter text field adjacent to the Action pop-up menu (the gear next to the Add button).

Actions you can perform on the Simulators pane include:

Add a simulator: Under the left column, click the Add button (+). In the sheet that appears, enter information about the simulator, then click Create.

Change the name of a simulator: In the left column, Control-click the simulator and choose Rename from the pop-up menu. Alternatively, double-click the name. Then enter the new name.

Delete a simulator from the list: Control-click the simulator and choose Delete from the pop-up menu. In the sheet that appears, click Delete. Alternatively, select the simulator and press the Delete key.

Show or hide a simulator as a run destination in the Scheme menu: Select the simulator, then in the detail area, select Show as run destination.

Filter the list of simulators: Enter text in the filter text field adjacent to the Action pop-up menu (the gear next to the Add button).
