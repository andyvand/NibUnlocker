Configure HealthKit (iOS, watchOS)

HealthKit allows your app to read and write health and activity user data that appears in the Health app. You must enable HealthKit to use the HealthKit framework. Then optionally enable access to health records.

Enable HealthKit and access health records

  1. In the project editor, select the target, then click Capabilities.

  2. In the HealthKit section, click the switch to turn it from OFF to ON.

  3. To enable access to health records, click the Health Records checkbox.

    To learn more about accessing health records, go to Health and Fitness Samples.
