Control the execution of your running app

The debug bar in the debug area lets you control the execution of your running app. The debug area automatically opens when you build and run your app.

The debug bar contains a button to disable or enable all breakpoints, a button to pause or continue execution of your app, and buttons to control the execution of your app.

  1. In the debug area, click the Pause button or wait for your app to stop at a breakpoint.

  2. Click the Step over button to evaluate the current line and continue to the next line.

  3. If your app is stopped at a function call, click the Step into button to step inside the function and continue there.

  4. Click the Step out button to continue execution until the next function return statement is called or the stack frame is popped, and step out of the current function to where it was called.

  5. Click the Continue execution button to resume running the app.
