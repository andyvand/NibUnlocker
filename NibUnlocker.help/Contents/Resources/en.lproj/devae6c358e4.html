View crash or energy report statistics

After you select a crash or energy report in the organizer, you can view statistics about the report in the inspector.

View the information by device type: In the upper-right corner, choose Device Type from the pop-up menu.

View the information by operating system: In the upper-right corner, choose Operating System from the pop-up menu.
