Share user crash and energy data with developers

Apple can’t provide crash or energy reports if users don’t share the data and statistics with app developers. If a user reports a crash and you don’t have a corresponding crash report, ask the user to share the crash data with app developers. Crash and energy data is automatically sent if you distribute an app using TestFlight but not if you distribute an app through the App Store.

Share iOS and watchOS data with developers

  1. In Settings, tap Privacy.

  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Analytics.

  3. If necessary, tap the Share With App Developers switch to enable it.

Share macOS data with developers

  1. In System Preferences, click Security & Privacy.

  2. Click Privacy and in the left column, click Diagnostics & Usage.

  3. Select “Share crash data with app developers.”

    To enable the Privacy options, click the lock icon. In the dialog that appears, enter the credentials for a system account with admin privileges and click Unlock.

Share tvOS data with developers

  1. In Settings, click General and click Privacy.

  2. Under Diagnostic Data, click “Share with App Developers.”

  3. In the dialog that appears, click “Send to Apple.”
