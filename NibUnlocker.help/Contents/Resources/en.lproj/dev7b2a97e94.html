Destination options

You select a destination option when you choose [Platform] App Store or Developer ID (macOS) as the distribution method.

For Developer ID, it is recommended that you choose Upload to notarize your app before you distribute it outside the Mac App Store.

Method

Description

Upload

For [Platform] App Store, uploads the app to App Store Connect for processing.

For Developer ID, uploads the app to Apple for processing. Later, when you export the notarized app, it is stapled with a ticket from Apple and signed with a Developer ID certificate. To learn more, go to Upload a macOS app to be notarized.

Export

For [Platform] App Store, signs the app with an App Store distribution certificate, then exports it.

For Developer ID, signs the app with a Developer ID certificate, then exports it.
