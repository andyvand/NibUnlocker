Customize the Touch Bar controls

Customize the Touch Bar controls for the source editor, Playground editor, Interface Builder, view debugger, and memory graph debugger.

  1. Open the desired editor or debugger.

  2. Choose View > Customize Touch Bar.

  3. Add, rearrange, or remove a button in the Touch Bar.

    Add a button: Drag a button from the screen to the Touch Bar; buttons in the Touch Bar jiggle slightly.

    Rearrange a button: On the Touch Bar, drag a button do the desired location.

    Remove a button: Drag a button from the Touch Bar to the screen.

  4. On the Touch Bar, tap Done.
