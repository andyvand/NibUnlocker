preview assistant

Use the Assistant editor preview assistant to view your layout on different iOS devices, in different orientations, at different split view sizes, and in different localizations.

Add (+) button: Add a preview for an iOS device.

Language button: Change the language of all previews or of the selected preview.

Note: The name of the language button can be the name of the default development language, of the the selected preview, or “Multiple Languages” if there are previews in different languages.

Orientation button (): Toggle the rotation between portrait and landscape.

To show the button, move the mouse over the preview or select the preview.

Note: You can preview only the localizations supported by your app.
