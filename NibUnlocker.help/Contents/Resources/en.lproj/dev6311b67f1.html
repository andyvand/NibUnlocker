Manage simulator runtimes and toolchains

Use Components preferences to download simulator runtimes and manage installed toolchains. To open Components preferences, choose Xcode > Preferences, then click Components.

Note: The Toolchains pane in Components preferences is visible only when an alternative toolchain is installed.
