View, delete, and install provisioning profiles on devices

Use the Devices and Simulators window to verify if a provisioning profile is installed on a device or to delete an expired or invalid provisioning profile from a device. Also, if you belong to multiple teams, you might want to remove older provisioning profiles from previous projects.

You rarely need to install a provisioning profile on a device yourself because when you launch an app on a device, the OS automatically installs the embedded provisioning profile in the app’s bundle on the device.

View provisioning profiles on a device

  1. If necessary, connect the device to your Mac. For watchOS apps, connect the iPhone paired with an Apple Watch.

  2. Open the Devices and Simulators window, then click Devices.

  3. In the left column, Control-click the device and choose Show Provisioning Profiles from the pop-up menu.

    A sheet appears showing all the provisioning profiles installed on the device.

  4. Click Done.

Delete a provisioning profile from a device

  1. Open the Devices and Simulators window, then click Devices.

  2. In the left column, Control-click the device and choose Show Provisioning Profiles from the pop-up menu.

  3. In the sheet that appears, select a provisioning profile from the list and in the lower-left corner, click the Delete button (–).

  4. Click Done.

Install a provisioning profile on a device

  1. Open the Devices and Simulators window, then click Devices.

  2. In the left column, Control-click the device and choose Show Provisioning Profiles from the pop-up menu.

  3. In the lower-left corner of the sheet, click the Add button (+).

  4. Choose the provisioning profile and click Install.

  5. Click Done.
