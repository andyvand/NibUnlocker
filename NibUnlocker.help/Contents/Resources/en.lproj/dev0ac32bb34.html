About the General pane

Use the General pane of the project editor to adjust general settings for the targets in your project, such as identity, signing, and deployment options.

Edit the general target settings

  1. In the Project navigator, select a project.

  2. In the left column of the project editor, select a target.

    To display the left column, click the “Show project and targets list” icon () or choose a target from the pop-up menu at the top of the project editor.

  3. Click General at the top of the project editor.

  4. If necessary, click the disclosure triangle icon () to expand or collapse a group of settings.
