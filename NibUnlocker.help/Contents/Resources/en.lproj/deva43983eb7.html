Configure Apple Pay (iOS, watchOS)

Apple Pay allows users to securely pay for physical goods and services using payment information stored in their device. You can enable Apple Pay and create a merchant identifier using Xcode. Then create a payment processing certificate using your developer account.

Alternatively, you can configure Apple Pay using your developer account.

Enable Apple Pay

  1. In the project editor, select the target, then click Capabilities.

  2. In the Apple Pay section, click the switch to turn it from OFF to ON.

Create a merchant identifier

  1. In the Apple Pay section, click the Add button (+) at the bottom of the Merchant IDs table.

  2. In the dialog that appears, enter the merchant identifier name, then click OK.
