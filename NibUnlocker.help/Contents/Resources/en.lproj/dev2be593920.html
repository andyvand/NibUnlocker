Configure keychain sharing

Enabling keychain sharing allows your app to share passwords in the keychain with other apps developed by your team.

Enable keychain sharing

  1. In the project editor, choose a target, then click Capabilities.

  2. In the Keychain Sharing section, click the switch to turn it from OFF to ON.

    Xcode adds the keychain-access-groups key to the entitlements file (a file with a .entitlements file extension).

Limit an app to a set of keychain access groups

Optionally, limit your app to a set of keychain access groups.

  1. In the Capabilities pane, click the Keychain Sharing disclosure triangle to reveal the settings.

  2. At the bottom of the Keychain Groups table, click the Add button (+).

  3. Double-click the placeholder text in the table, and enter the keychain access group you want to add.

Delete a keychain access group
