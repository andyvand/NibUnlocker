Configure HomeKit (iOS, tvOS, watchOS)

HomeKit allows your app to communicate with and control connected accessories in a user’s home. HomeKit provides a standard way to communicate with those accessories and create home configurations. Use HomeKit Accessory Simulator to test the communication of your HomeKit app to simulated accessories.

Enable HomeKit

  1. In the project editor, select the target, then click Capabilities.

  2. In the HomeKit section, click the switch to turn it from OFF to ON.

Download HomeKit Accessory Simulator

  1. In the Capabilities pane, click the HomeKit disclosure triangle to reveal the settings.

  2. Click Download HomeKit Accessory Simulator.

  3. In a browser, locate and download the “Additional Tools for Xcode 8" DMG file.

  4. In the Finder, Double-click the DMG file in ~/Downloads.

  5. Drag HomeKit Accessory Simulator (located in the Hardware folder) to the /Applications folder.

Alternatively, choose Xcode > Open Developer Tool > More Developer Tools to download HomeKit Accessory Simulator.
