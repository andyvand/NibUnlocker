Run, cancel, and delete integrations

In the Report navigator, you can run, delete, or cancel an integration.

Run an integration

There are multiple ways to manually run an integration.

  • In the left column, select a bot and in the upper-right corner, click the Integrate button.

  • In the left column, Control-click a bot and from the pop-up menu, choose Integrate.

  • In the left column, select a bot and from the gear menu, choose Integrate.

Alternatively, you can schedule integrations when you create a bot or on the Schedule pane, when you edit a bot).

Cancel or delete an integration

There are multiple ways to cancel or delete an integration.

  • In the left column, Control-click an integration and from the pop-up menu, choose Cancel Integration or Delete Integration.

    If Cancel Integration is disabled, the integration is not running.

  • In the left column, select an integration and from the gear menu, choose Cancel Integration or Delete Integration.
