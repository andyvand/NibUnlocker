particle emitters

A particle emitter is a function of the SpriteKit framework that allows you to specify a point in the particle’s display and create images that move and change over time. You can simulate rain, snow, fire, and many other effects in your game. Particle emitters provide a quick and efficient way to add special effects to SpriteKit apps. Emitters can range from a single image that barely moves to thousands of small particles flying around the screen.

In your code, you can access a particle emitter’s properties with the SKEmitterNode class.
