Upload in-app purchase content to App Store Connect

You can have Apple host non-consumable in-app purchase products and upload the in-app purchase content to App Store Connect using Xcode. Before you begin, create an in-app purchase record associated with your app and turn on content hosting in App Store Connect.

Note: To create an in-app purchase content project, select the In-App Purchase Content template under Cross-platform when you create your Xcode project. Then add the content to the project, described in In-App Purchase Programming Guide.

Upload in-app purchase content

  1. In the main window, choose the in-app purchase content target from the Scheme menu in the toolbar.

  2. Choose Product > Archive.

  3. In the Archives organizer, select the in-app purchase content archive, then click “Distribute Content.”

  4. In the sheet that appears, select Submit In-App Purchase Content, then click Next.

  5. In the next sheet, choose a team, then click Next.

  6. In the next sheet, choose an app from the App Record pop-up menu, then click Next.

  7. In the next sheet, review the contents of the package, then click Upload.

  8. After the upload completes, click Done.
