Manage CloudKit schemas and records

Use the CloudKit Dashboard web app to manage your CloudKit container schema and records.

  1. Under the Containers table, click the CloudKit Dashboard button.

  2. Sign in to CloudKit Dashboard using your Apple ID.

  3. Click a container, then choose an area in the Development or Production environment.
