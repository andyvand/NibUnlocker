Balance and identify matching braces, parentheses, and brackets while editing your code

Xcode helps you identify and balance beginning and ending delimiters—braces, parentheses, and brackets—within your code.

Automatically insert a closing delimiter: Type an opening delimiter. You can disable this functionality in Text Editing preferences.

Briefly highlight an opening delimiter: Type the closing delimiter or use the Right Arrow key to move the insertion point past a closing delimiter.

Briefly highlight a closing delimiter: Use the Right Arrow key to move the insertion point past an opening delimiter.

Select the opening delimiter, closing delimiter, and the code in-between: Double-click the opening or closing delimiter, or click between the delimiters and choose Editor > Structure > Balance Delimiter.
