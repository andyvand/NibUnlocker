view debugger controls

Use the view controls to change how the view hierarchy is rendered on the debugger canvas.

Task

Steps

Rotate the rendered views.

Drag in the canvas.

Change the spacing between the rendered view layers.

Drag the the lower left slider.

Show clipped content for the selected view.

Click the Show clipped content button ().

Show Auto Layout constraints for the selected view.

Click the Show constraints button ().

Toggle between 2D and 3D views.

Click the Orient to 2D/3D button (/) .

Display only the contents of the views.

Choose Contents () from the view mode pop-up menu.

Display only the wireframes of the views.

Choose Wireframes () from the view mode pop-up menu.

Display the contents and wireframes of the views.

Choose Wireframes and Contents () from the view mode pop-up menu.

Change the bottommost visible view.

Move the left handle of the double ended slider on the lower right.

Change the topmost visible view.

Move the right handle of the double ended slider on the lower right.

Change the magnification of the displayed views.

Click the Zoom In (+) and Zoom Out (-) buttons. Click the Actual Size button (=) to show the views at full size.
